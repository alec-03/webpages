Hello! My name is Alec. Welcome to my website!

I keep myself busy by thinking about how we can use robots to extend our humanity. What are the most robotic aspects of our lives that robots ought to do? How can we extend human capabilities with robotic aid? These are the kinds of meta-problems that I want to solve.
